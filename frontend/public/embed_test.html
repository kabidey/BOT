Mackertich ONE — embed widget smoke test

This page exists at /embed_test.html on our preview host. It loads /widget.js (also from this host) and is the manual end-to-end test for the floating chat bubble.

You should see a gold-bordered circular bubble in the bottom-right corner. Click it to open the iframe-mounted chat. Send a message, then click the × button in the chat header to minimise back to the bubble — your conversation should persist when you re-open it.

To test the left-side variant, append ?left=1 to the URL.
