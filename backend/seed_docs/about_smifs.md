# About Mackertich ONE — the wealth-management vertical of SMIFS Ltd

## Who We Are
Mackertich ONE is the wealth-management vertical of **SMIFS Ltd**, serving high-net-worth individuals, ultra-high-net-worth families, corporate treasuries, and institutional clients across India. Backed by SMIFS Ltd's capital-markets heritage, the Mackertich ONE practice combines disciplined research, transparent fee structures, and a long-term partnership model designed to preserve and compound family capital across generations.

## Our Philosophy
At Mackertich ONE we approach wealth management as a stewardship discipline, not a transaction business. Three principles guide every client engagement:

- **Capital preservation precedes capital growth.** A portfolio that avoids permanent loss compounds more reliably than one chasing the latest theme.
- **Asset allocation, not security selection, drives long-term outcomes.** We invest substantial research bandwidth in the strategic and tactical allocation decisions that explain over 80% of return variability.
- **Transparency is non-negotiable.** Every fee, every commission, every conflict of interest is disclosed in writing before any product recommendation.

## Services We Offer
Through Mackertich ONE, clients access the full spectrum of SMIFS Ltd's wealth services:

- **Portfolio Management Services (PMS)** — Curated discretionary and non-discretionary portfolios across thematic, multi-cap, and concentrated mandates.
- **Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs)** — Category I, II, and III allocations sourced from established managers with multi-cycle track records.
- **Mutual Fund Advisory** — Direct Plan execution with research-driven scheme selection across equity, debt, and hybrid categories.
- **Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) & Fixed Income** — Primary and secondary market participation in corporate debt.
- **Initial Public Offerings (IPOs)** — Curated primary market participation with rigorous filtering.
- **Estate Planning & Family Trusts** — Coordination with legal partners on trust structures, succession planning, and inter-generational transfer.
- **Loan Against Securities** — Liquidity solutions for clients holding listed equity, mutual fund, or bond portfolios.

## Client Profile
A typical Mackertich ONE engagement begins at an investable surplus of ₹2 crore and scales to family-office mandates of ₹100 crore and above. We maintain a deliberately limited client roster to ensure each family receives senior-team attention and a customised investment policy statement.

## Investment Process
Every Mackertich ONE relationship begins with a comprehensive financial diagnosis covering current assets, liabilities, cash-flow profile, family goals, tax position, and risk profile. The output is a written Investment Policy Statement (IPS) that governs portfolio construction, rebalancing triggers, and review cadence. We meet clients at least quarterly and revise the IPS upon material life events.

## Regulatory Standing
Mackertich ONE operates as the wealth-management vertical of **SMIFS Ltd**, which is registered with the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) and operates within the regulatory frameworks applicable to its respective service lines. All client assets are held in segregated demat and bank accounts in the client's own name.
